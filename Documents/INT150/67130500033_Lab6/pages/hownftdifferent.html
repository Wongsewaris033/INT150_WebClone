How is an NFT Different From Other Cryptocurrencies?

Although NFTs are created using the same kind of programming language as other cryptocurrencies, that's where the similarity ends.

Other Cryptocurrency NFT
Cryptocurrencies are “fungible”; they can
be traded or exchanged for one another.
They’re also equal in value. 		Each NFT acts as a digital signature that
makes it impossible for them to be
exchanged for or equal to one another.
For example, one Bitcoin is always equal
to another Bitcoin, or one Dollar is
always equal to one Dollar. 		For example, The Last Supper is a painting
of a kind and cannot be exchanged with
another painting.
